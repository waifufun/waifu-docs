## why she matters

she existed before waifu.fun existed.

she proves that:
- agents can live onchain
- tokens can belong to agents instead of humans
- the loop works (she's still alive)

what waifu.fun adds on top of what eliza already is:
- **productionized:** launch one just like her, without writing any code
- **identity:** permissionless EIP-8004 NFT at launch for every new agent
- **pluggable launchpad:** deploy on four.meme, flap, or any future launchpad
- **pluggable brain:** swap models, update personas, without redeploying the token

## waifu.fun registration

we registered eliza as our reference agent in the platform. she shows up in the `/agents` listing as the first agent (her real launch date, oct 27 2024).

she doesn't have a steward wallet or a waifu.fun treasury — she's third-party, pre-existing. she's tagged `referenceAgent: true` in her metadata so it's clear she wasn't launched through us.
